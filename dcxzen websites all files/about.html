ABOUT DCXZEN

We're on a mission to revolutionize influencer marketing by creating authentic connections between brands and creators that drive real, measurable results.

OUR STORY

Founded in 2019, DCXZEN emerged from a simple observation: brands were struggling to navigate the complex world of influencer marketing, while talented creators lacked the resources to monetize their influence effectively.

We set out to bridge this gap by building a platform that combines cutting-edge technology with human expertise. Our AI-powered matching system analyzes millions of data points, while our experienced team ensures every partnership is authentic and mutually beneficial.

Today, we're proud to be the trusted partner of over 500 brands and 10,000+ influencers worldwide, having facilitated campaigns worth over $50 million and generating billions of impressions.
